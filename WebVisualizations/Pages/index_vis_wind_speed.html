Wind Speed

The data on wind speed is not convincing enough to say that it is correlated with latitude. In the southern hemisphere, we observed a very weak negative correlation. In the northern hemisphere, we observed a very weak positive correlation. We do see some of the higher wind speed cities further away from 0 degrees latitude, particularly above 60 degrees. However, there are many cities far away from the equator that have low winds as well. Perhaps other factors, such as air pressure, may be more correlated with wind speed.
